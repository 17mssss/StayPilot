# 🎬 Prompt Hyperframes — Vidéo Publicitaire StayPilot

> **Comment l'utiliser :**
> 1. Télécharge `claude-design-hyperframes.md` sur GitHub et attache-le à ton chat Claude Design
> 2. Ou dans Claude Code : lance d'abord `/hyperframes` puis colle le prompt ci-dessous
> 3. Itère avec des petites corrections ciblées (ne re-prompte pas from scratch)

---

## VERSION A — LinkedIn / YouTube (16:9, 30 secondes)

```
/hyperframes

Use the attached skill. Create a 30-second product launch video for StayPilot.

**Product:** StayPilot — AI-powered property management platform for short-term rental hosts (Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Abritel).
**Target:** French-speaking Airbnb hosts tired of wasting hours on messages, reviews, and logistics.
**Format:** 16:9 (1920×1080), 30 seconds, 6 scenes
**Style:** Apple-inspired minimalism — near-black background (#0d0d0f), white and off-white typography, primary accent rose-to-indigo gradient. Mood: premium, confident, effortless.
**Motion:** Smooth entrances on most elements. Snappy transitions between scenes. Dramatic easing on the product name reveal.
**Transitions:** Whip pan between feature scenes for energy. Warm blur crossfade into the final CTA.
**Caption tone:** Corporate for narration lines, Hype for feature labels.

---

**TTS Narration** (voice: am_michael, speed: 1.0x — professional male, calm authority):
"Gérer vos locations courte durée prend des heures chaque jour.
Messages sans fin. Avis à rédiger. Logistique permanente.
StayPilot automatise tout — IA, inbox, livrets, avis — en une seule plateforme.
Rejoignez 200 hôtes qui ont repris leur temps.
Commencez à 59 euros par mois."

---

**Scene 1 — Le Problème (0–6s)**
Background: near-black. Subtle noise texture overlay.
Large headline fades in slowly (dreamy easing, 1.2s):
  "Vous perdez des heures chaque jour
   à gérer vos locations."
Below it, three tags appear sequentially with snappy slide-up (0.15s stagger):
  [ MESSAGES ] [ AVIS ] [ LOGISTIQUE ]
Tags styled with thin border, rose accent color, 14px uppercase tracking-wide.
Add bass-driven scale pulse (8% intensity) to the tags on beat at 2s.

**Scene 2 — La Solution (6–11s)**
Whip pan transition from Scene 1.
Center: StayPilot wordmark appears with dramatic zoom-through (expo.out).
Below: tagline with slow fade (storytelling tone):
  "L'IA qui gère à votre place."
Add a marker highlight sweep in rose on "L'IA" (highlight mode).
Subtle radiant glow emanates from the wordmark (treble-reactive, 15% intensity).

**Scene 3a — Feature: Inbox Unifié (11–14s)**
Split into left text column + right visual panel.
Left: "Inbox Unifié" label (hype tone, 72px bold, scale-pop entrance).
     Sub-label: "Airbnb · Booking · WhatsApp · SMS · Email"
Right: 5 channel icons slide in from right with snappy easing, staggered 0.08s each.
     Each icon has a colored dot badge (rose=Airbnb, blue=Booking, green=WhatsApp…).
Caption overlay (corporate tone, fade+slide): "Tous vos messages, un seul endroit."

**Scene 3b — Feature: Messagerie IA (14–17s)**
Push slide transition.
Two chat bubbles appear from bottom — guest message (light bg), then AI suggestion bubble (purple gradient).
AI badge "✦ Suggestion IA" bounces in above the purple bubble (bouncy easing).
Hype caption, scale-pop: "RÉPONSE EN 1 CLIC"
Sub-caption (corporate fade): "L'IA rédige, vous validez."

**Scene 3c — Feature: Livrets & Avis (17–20s)**
Split screen with push slide.
Left half: QR code reveals with circular wipe animation.
  Label (social tone, bounce): "Livret QR → envoyé par email"
Right half: Star rating row fills from left (5 stars, rose color, each star scale-pops 0.1s apart).
  Label (hype tone): "5★ EN AUTOMATIQUE"

**Scene 4 — Social Proof (20–24s)**
Warm blur crossfade.
Dark background. Center: large animated counter ticks up from 150 to 200 (snappy, 1.5s duration).
  "+200 hôtes nous font confiance"
Counter number: 96px bold white. Add bass-driven scale pulse on each digit tick (6% intensity).
Below: three small avatar circles (gradient initials, staggered fade-in).
Corporate caption: "Starter · Pro · Business — Sans engagement"

**Scene 5 — CTA (24–30s)**
Zoom-through transition.
Full-screen gradient background: rose-500 → indigo-600 diagonal.
Bold white headline (80px, dramatic entrance, expo.out):
  "Commencez à 59€/mois"
Add hand-drawn circle around "59€" (circle mode, white color).
Below: URL in clean monospace, fade-in:
  "staypilot.app"
Small CTA pill button "Essai gratuit →" bounces in at 27s (bouncy easing, white bg, dark text).
Last 1s: slow fade to black.

---

**Rendering:** high quality
**Audio:** TTS narration mixed at 80% volume. Subtle background music: ambient, minimal, slightly cinematic. Mix music at 20%.
```

## VERSION B — TikTok / Instagram Reels (9:16, 20 secondes)

```
/hyperframes

Use the attached skill. Create a 20-second vertical TikTok-style hook video for StayPilot.

**Format:** 9:16 (1080×1920), 20 seconds, hook-first structure
**Style:** High-energy social — dark background, bold typography, snappy animations. Mood: punchy, relatable.
**Motion:** Bouncy and snappy throughout. Fast cuts every 3–4 seconds.
**Transitions:** Zoom through between scenes for TikTok energy.
**Caption tone:** Hype for everything — scale-pop, large, impossible to miss.

---

**TTS Narration** (voice: af_sky, speed: 1.1x — energetic female voice):
"T'es encore à répondre à tes Airbnb à minuit ?
StayPilot fait tout à ta place.
Inbox unifié, IA qui répond, livrets automatiques.
200 hôtes ont arrêté de galérer.
59 euros par mois. Essaie maintenant."

---

**Scene 1 — Hook (0–4s)**
Giant bold text, center screen (hype, 88px):
  "T'es encore à répondre
   à tes Airbnb à minuit ? 😅"
Text bounces in from bottom (bouncy easing).
Add burst lines around "minuit" (burst mode, rose color).
Subtle noise grain overlay for authenticity.

**Scene 2 — Solution (4–8s)**
Zoom-through transition.
StayPilot logo + wordmark, dramatic scale entrance.
  "StayPilot fait tout à ta place."
Rose underline animates under "tout" (marker highlight sweep).
Hype caption pop-in: "IA · INBOX · LIVRETS · AVIS"
Tags slide in from sides, alternating left/right.

**Scene 3 — Features rapides (8–15s, 3 micro-scenes de 2.5s)**
Each micro-scene: full-width card, black bg, single feature highlight.

  3a. 📥 "INBOX UNIFIÉ"
      5 channel dots pulse in sequence (Airbnb rose, Booking blue, WhatsApp green…)
      Caption (social bounce): "Tous tes messages en 1 endroit"

  3b. 🤖 "IA QUI RÉPOND"
      Chat bubble with ✦ badge pops in (bouncy).
      Caption: "Tu valides en 1 clic"

  3c. ⭐ "5★ AUTOMATIQUE"
      5 stars fill with scale-pop (staggered 0.12s).
      Caption: "Fini les avis oubliés"

**Scene 4 — CTA (15–20s)**
Full rose-to-indigo gradient background.
Bold white text, dramatic zoom-in:
  "59€/mois"
Hand-drawn circle around price (circle mode, white).
Below, bounce-in:
  "staypilot.app"
  "→ Essai gratuit"
Final frame holds 1s then fades.

---

**Rendering:** high quality
**Audio:** TTS narration at 85%. Upbeat background loop at 15%.
```

## Conseils d'itération (après le premier rendu)

Utilise ces commandes courtes pour affiner — ne re-prompte jamais from scratch :

- `"Make the StayPilot wordmark 1.5x bigger in Scene 2"`
- `"Add a lower third at 3s with 'Géré par l'IA' in rose color"`
- `"Speed up the feature scenes — make each 2s instead of 3s"`
- `"Replace the background music with assets/music.mp3"`
- `"Make the price counter in Scene 4 pulse more aggressively — 12% bass intensity"`
- `"Add karaoke-style word highlighting to the narration captions"`
- `"Swap Scene 1 background to a dark navy instead of near-black"`
- `"Render at draft quality first for fast preview"`
